Love me!

Please understand that many of these pets have been abused or abandoned. All of them are pets that are confused and frightened. My goal is to not only restore their health, but to also restore their trust in human beings.. Some pets cannot be saved, but they still should be given the opportunity to leave this world in peace, comfort and happiness. This will, in many cases, be the only time they have felt loved.
